about the archive

this is a map of a place that doesn't exist. a ghost town built from the people i once loved and a life i only dare to dream about.

these are the things that didn't fit into conversations, the thoughts that didn't belong to the people i knew or the places i've been.

for a long time, i didn't either.

if you find these fragments unpolished or confusing, it is because they are. they are the raw spills of a world i'm still learning to put into sentences. if you've found your way here, you are a guest in a room i usually keep locked. stay as long as you need. maybe something here belongs to you, too.
